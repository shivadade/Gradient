Indian Hockey Team for Tokyo Olympics 2020

P.R Sreejesh As the seniormost and most experienced player in the team, Sreejesh will be vital to the team’s performance in Tokyo in his third Olympics. Captain in 2016 and the lone goalkeeper in the side, the 33-year-old from Kerala made his international debut in 2006 at the South Asian Games in Sri Lanka. He had to wait till the Asian Champions Trophy in 2011 to break into the spotlight but since then has been the first choice over years. A crowd favourite for his spontaneous interactions with the public, his flexibility, reading of the game and ability to stay non-committal till the end are considered his biggest strengths on field and remains the team’s biggest motivator off it.
